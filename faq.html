FAQ

How does it work?

Once you connect your apps, all generated content such as chat history, documents, spreadsheets, presentations, brochures, images & videos to name a few, gets supported by your OneBrain AI. As you continue to work, the AI assistant continues to gain your additional knowledge base.

What if the OneBrain AI makes a mistake?

Although in limited cases, this usually means you or someone else are actively also making these mistakes, as the AI is going over past responses of answers between people and the materials they make. But no worries! This allows you to catch bad practices. Just tag the mistake and the admin responds to the AI for correction.

How much does it cost?

We are currently testing for our beta release. This information will be public once testing is finalized. However, OneBrain will have a fairly low pricing plan in order to support all income classes to benefit and compete in the AI revolution.

What if you’re missing an app I use?

No worries! You can fill in a app API request here. Our plan is to support all apps big or small that our customers want to use. If you are the owner of a new app and you want OneBrain integrated to it, you can fill this form out here.

Is OneBrain secure?

OneBrain is not trained with any data, it uses queries through key words to find the information you request and articulate the information through natural language processing. Deleting the chat, removes it from the OneBrain’s server. OneBrain AI will only have access if the user has access. Therefore anyone can keep their current security of choice on how to protect their data. it’s just a tool to help access the data you already have permissions too through email.

Can I integrate my Onebrain chat bot in any app?

Not all apps. Only apps that have a user interface for conversational text such as Slack, Discord and Asana. Exceptions are Google Drive and Google Analytics whereas the user interface does not support chat bot discussions. All permissioned users however will be able to sign into their account on OneBrain and use our own text editor for your AI communication. Stay tuned for the release of our beta!

If I just have general questions such as for press or partnerships, who should I contact?

We’d love to talk to you! Reach out to us by email at hello@onebrain.io. One of our team members will get back to you.
